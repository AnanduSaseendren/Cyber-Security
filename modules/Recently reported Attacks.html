Recently reported Attacks

  1. Welltok Healthcare Data Breach (November 2023): Sensitive data of approximately 8.5 million individuals was exposed in a breach targeting the healthcare industry. The breach involved private health information and financial details
  2. Coin Cloud Financial Services Attack (November 2023): A breach impacted 300,000 customers, leaking sensitive financial data. The incident is a significant example of the vulnerabilities in financial technology systems
  3. University of Michigan Breach (November 2023): Academic institutions are increasingly targeted, as seen in this breach where 63,000 individuals’ data was compromised
  4. Bluewater Health Data Leak (November 2023): Over 5.6 million patient records were exposed, emphasizing the healthcare sector's continued vulnerability
  5. MoveIt Vulnerability Exploitation: Earlier in 2024, the Clop ransomware group exploited a vulnerability in the Progress MoveIt file transfer tool, leading to widespread breaches affecting numerous organizations globally​
